Living and creating

... in Saint-Petersburg.
My favorite genre is CITYSCAPE because I am in love with the city with its daily bustle, endless labyrinths of streets and garlands of night lanterns. Life in the city is the main leitmotif of my paintings.
In my gallery you can enjoy night Venice, sitting in an open-air cafe on the Grand Canal, have a cup of aromatic coffee in Montmartre, or take a stroll along Amsterdam’s main promenade. Or perhaps, leave your bike;) and you will want to wander on foot along the Seine.
I wish you a pleasant viewing and have a good mood !!!
